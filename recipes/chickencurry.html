Chicken curry recipe by piyu

Description :

Chicken curry recipe by piyu (twin)

Ingredients :

  • Chicken
  • Chilli powder
  • Curd
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Green chilli
  • GG paste

Steps:

  1. First marinate the chicken with curd, chilli powder, salt.
  2. In a pan add "lil" (i have no idea what she means by lil) bit of oil and fry the ginger garlic paste in low flame.
  3. Add onions and once it's translucent add the chicken.
  4. Cover and cook for ten minutes.
  5. Now add tomatoes and chillies to the chicken and again cover and cook for ten minutes, add some water if it's dry.
  6. Garnish it with some coriander.

Enjoy yo chicken curry!!
